Freedom?

You're asking me about freedom?!

You're asking ME about freedom? Ha.

I'll be honest with you... I know a whole more about what freedom isn't than about what it is, because I've never been free.

I can only share my vision with you of the future, about what freedom is

The way I see it, freedom is... is the right to grow, is the right to Blossom.

Freedom is... is the right to be yourself, to be who you are, to be who you wanna be, to do what you wanna do...

- Sister Shakur of Anon
